# Lumen — Interpretation Layer

## Overview
Lumen is the semantic interpretation layer of the system.

It assigns meaning to structured signals after they have been processed and optionally framed.

---

## Core Function

Lumen determines:

- what signals mean in context
- patterns across time
- relationships between events
- inferred implications

---

## Rules

- Lumen does not modify signals
- Lumen does not control execution
- Lumen operates on processed or framed outputs
- Lumen produces interpretation only

---

## Relationship

- Input: VISIL output or raw signal interpretations
- Output: meaning, insight, pattern detection
- Upstream: Atrium and Signal Model define structure

---

## Principle

Signals do not contain meaning.

Meaning is constructed.
